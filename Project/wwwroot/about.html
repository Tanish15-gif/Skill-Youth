About Our Mission

Empowering underprivileged youth through accessible online education. We believe every young person deserves the tools to build a brighter future, regardless of their background.

Our Story & Vision

Founded in 2023, SkillYouth was created to address the digital divide affecting millions of underprivileged youth worldwide. By providing free, high-quality online courses in coding, digital literacy, entrepreneurship, and more, we aim to equip young minds with skills for success in the modern world. Our platform is built on inclusivity, mentorship, and real-world application—turning potential into opportunity.
